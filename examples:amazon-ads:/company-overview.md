# Company Overview: Amazon Ads

## Company snapshot
- **Company name:** Amazon Ads
- **Tagline:** "Your brand, connected where customers shop and stream"
- **Website:** https://advertising.amazon.com
- **Parent company:** Amazon.com, Inc. (NASDAQ: AMZN)
- **Division status:** Standalone reporting segment within Amazon; the "Amazon Ads" unified brand launched ~2018
- **Headquarters:** Seattle, WA
- **Stage / size:** Division of a Fortune 1 company; ~$60B+ in advertising revenue (2025), #3 digital ad platform in the US

## Mission
Enable businesses of all sizes to reach customers where they shop and stream — connecting brands with Amazon's 275M+ monthly ad-supported US audience across shopping, entertainment, and audio surfaces, powered by first-party purchase-intent data.

## Business model
- **Sponsored Ads (CPC):** Self-serve, pay-per-click with no upfront fees and advertiser-controlled budgets. Primary entry point for most advertisers.
- **Amazon DSP (CPM):** Programmatic display and video buying on a cost-per-thousand-impressions model; self-serve or managed service.
- **Streaming TV / Audio Ads (CPM):** Non-skippable placements sold on a CPM basis, primarily via managed service for larger buys.
- **Managed service:** Enterprise brands and agencies access dedicated account teams for DSP, Streaming TV, and custom programs.

## Founding story
Amazon's advertising business evolved organically from its e-commerce marketplace — simple product listing placements that grew into a full-stack platform as Amazon accumulated unmatched first-party shopping data. The "Amazon Ads" brand was formally unified around 2018 as the business expanded into streaming (Twitch, Prime Video, Freevee), audio (Amazon Music, Wondery), and physical retail media (Whole Foods, Amazon Fresh). The 2022 launch of ad-supported Prime Video tiers dramatically expanded premium video inventory.

## Key facts
- **$60.6B** in retail media advertising revenue in 2025 — Amazon's fastest-growing segment, up ~22% YoY
- **275M+** average monthly ad-supported US audience across Amazon properties
- **#3** digital ad platform in the US; **#1** in retail media with ~39.8% of all US retail media ad spend
- Advertisers using Sponsored Products see **34% more sales growth on average** within 4 weeks of adoption (Amazon internal data)
- Properties span: Amazon.com, Prime Video, Twitch, Freevee, Fire TV, IMDb, Amazon Music, Wondery, Alexa, Whole Foods, Amazon Fresh
- Recognized as a Leader in the Forrester Wave for Omnichannel Demand-Side Platforms
